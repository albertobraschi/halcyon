h2. WeeDB

Source: "http://github.com/mtodd/halcyon/tree/master/examples/weedb/":http://github.com/mtodd/halcyon/tree/master/examples/weedb/

WeeDB is a clone of the "TinyDB":http://tinydb.org/ service. It is not
production quality code, but it is a good example of how to write a
